TRACKSIX

Athlete Pack

Event Overview

TRACKSIX is a 6-hour team relay endurance event held at Corby Athletics Running Club. Teams of 4 athletes take turns completing 1-mile loops on the track, rotating in a fixed order throughout the event.

Each athlete completes 4 laps (1 mile) before handing over to the next team member. The goal is simple — keep moving, keep your team rolling, and clock as many miles as possible in 6 hours.

Schedule

  • 7:30am — Registration opens
  • 8:30am — Registration closes / Athlete briefing
  • 9:00am — Gun start
  • 3:00pm — Race end (6-hour race)

Rules

  • Teams consist of 4 athletes
  • Each athlete completes 4 laps (1 mile) before changing over
  • Athletes must maintain their set rotation order throughout the event — order cannot be changed once the event has started
  • The relay baton must be physically handed over at each changeover — no exceptions
  • From lap 3 of each leg, only the active outgoing athlete and the incoming athlete due to change over are permitted in the changeover corral — all other team members must remain outside the corral area
  • All participants must attend the pre-race briefing at 8:30am
  • Participants must follow all instructions from event staff and volunteers at all times
  • If any one athlete withdraws from the event, the entire team is withdrawn — all four athletes must remain active throughout

Kit & Equipment

  • No headphones permitted during the event
  • Each team will be provided with a relay baton — this must be used at every changeover
  • Wear appropriate running kit and footwear for track conditions
